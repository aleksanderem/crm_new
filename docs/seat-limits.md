# Seat Limits

Every organization has a seat limit that controls how many team members can belong to the organization at one time.

## How Seat Limits Work

Each subscription plan includes a maximum number of seats. The seat count includes the organization owner and all team members, regardless of their role. When the seat limit is reached, no new members can be invited until a seat is freed (by removing a member) or the plan is upgraded.

Pending invitations do not count toward the seat limit. Only active team memberships are counted.

## Free Tier

Organizations on the free tier have a default limit of 5 seats. This includes the owner, so up to 4 additional members can be invited.

## Paid Plans

Paid plans offer higher seat limits depending on the plan tier. The exact limit is shown on the team settings page under "Current Usage." To see all available plans and their seat limits, visit Settings > Billing.

## How to Upgrade

When you reach your seat limit, an upgrade prompt appears on the team settings page. Click the upgrade button or navigate to Settings > Billing to change your plan. Once upgraded, the new seat limit takes effect immediately and additional members can be invited.

## Viewing Seat Usage

Navigate to Settings > Team to see a progress bar showing current seat usage. The bar changes color as you approach the limit: amber at 80% capacity and red when the limit is reached.
